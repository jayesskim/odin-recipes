Three Corn Casserole

Description

This Corn Casserole recipe, aka corn pudding, is a traditional Thanksgiving side dish. Itâ€™s a creamier, cheesier version of cornbread!

source: The Gunny Sack

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 1/4-ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 (14 3/4-ounce) can cream-style corn
  • 1 (8-ounce) package corn muffin mix (recommended: Jiffy)
  • 1 cup French onion dip
  • 1 stick of butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Put casserole dish with a half stick of butter in while the oven is preheating
  3. Melt the other half stick of butter in the microwave
  4. Beat 3 eggs until foamy
  5. Mix the eggs, French onion dip, and half a stick of melted butter
  6. Stir in 1 can of kernel corn (drained) and 1 can of cream-style corn
  7. Stir in 1 package of corn muffin mix
  8. Take the casserole dish out of the oven and brush the butter along to sides of the dish
  9. Pour in the mixture and mix until butter is mixed in (should be homogeneous)
  10. Bake for 50-60 minutes at 350F
  11. Let it settle for 5-10 minutes
